About the Toolkit

The Toolkit has been under constant development since 1997. From that time, it has undergone three massive versions, and changed from one man's hobby to an open source project. Many sites devoted the system, and a tightly-knit community have been born. In this section, we'll show you the recent changes to version 3, and provide links to relevant sites.
